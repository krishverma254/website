RIVER

Rivers provide fresh water for drinking, agriculture, and industry. They support ecosystems, offer habitats for wildlife, and help in transportation and trade. Additionally, rivers generate hydropower and promote tourism through recreational activities.

TREE

Trees are essential for life as they produce oxygen and clean the air. They help regulate the climate, conserve water, and prevent soil erosion. Trees also provide shelter, food, and resources like fruits, timber, and medicines.
